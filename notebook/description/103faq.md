# about us page
- inspired by the About Us page of Salmon Books, a Thai publishing company. The content is adapted to fit the context of our book recommendation system, "Greed Route".


## task
- create a FAQ page for "Greed Route" book recommendation system, using the above example as reference. The content should be adapted to fit the context of our book recommendation system, and should include relevant information about frequently asked questions regarding orders, payments, and other common inquiries from users.

## list of questions to be included in the FAQ page
- How long does it take to receive the recommended books?
- What should I do if I receive the wrong book, a duplicate, a damaged book, or if I don't receive the book at all?
- How can I check the status of my book recommendations?
- Can I cancel my book recommendation or return a book after receiving it?
- How can I contact customer support for further assistance?
- Are there any special offers or discounts available for users of Greed Route?
- How does Greed Route ensure the quality of book recommendations?
