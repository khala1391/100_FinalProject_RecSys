## description
- The News and Interview page of the website serves as a platform for users to stay updated on the latest news, interviews, and articles related to books, authors, and the literary world. It typically includes a header with navigation links to key sections of the site, a main content area that showcases news articles and interviews, and sidebars that offer additional options and features. The News and Interview page is designed to keep users informed about the latest happenings in the book industry, allowing them to easily access news articles, author interviews, and other relevant content that aligns with their interests. It provides a space for users to stay connected with the literary community and discover new insights and perspectives on books and authors.

## details

- **left sidebar**: shortcuts link to different sections of the News and Interview page, e.g. latest news, author interviews, book reviews, etc. with hyperlinks to the respective sections of the News and Interview page
  - Latest news: show the latest news articles related to books, authors, and the literary world, with options to filter by genre, author, publication date, etc.
  - Author interviews: show interviews with authors about their latest books, writing process, inspirations, etc., with options to filter by genre, author, publication date, etc.
  - Book reviews: show reviews of new book releases by critics and readers, with options to filter by genre, author, publication date, etc.

- **body**:
  - show news articles and interviews in a visually appealing layout, with options to filter and sort the content based on user preferences and interests. Each news article or interview should include a headline, a brief summary, and a link to the full article or interview for users to read more.
  - *style*: start with stacked layout, which can be switched to grid view with a button. In stacked layout, each news article or interview is displayed in a vertical stack, while in grid view, the articles and interviews are displayed in a grid format with cover images, headlines, and brief summaries.
  - end with pagination or infinite scroll to allow users to easily navigate through the news articles and interviews and discover more content that aligns with their interests.
    - example: `https://baseweb.design/components/pagination/`
